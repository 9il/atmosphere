    atmosphere

    Statistical package.

    Testing in progress. NOT READY FOR PRODUCTION!

    Numeric methods

    1. Solvs optimization problem f(p) -> min, where p is discrete probability distribution with k elements.
    f(p) -> min
f = u(Wp),
W - matrix(n rows, k columns),
u - convex function.
    1. Separates normal variance mean mixtures.
